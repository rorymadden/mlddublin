About MLD

Manual lymphatic drainage (MLD) is a type of gentle massage with rhythmic circular movements intended to stimulate the natural drainage of the lymph, which carries waste products away from the tissues back toward the heart.

MLD aims to redirect fluid from swollen areas to healthy lymphatic vessels, transporting it back to the normal circulatory system.

About Bernadette
